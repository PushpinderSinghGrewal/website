About Us

The Sardar Vallabhbhai National Institute of Technology, Surat (NIT Surat), perceives to be a globally accepted centre of excellence in technical education catalysing the transfer of high technologies resulting in enhanced quality. Research and Incubation cell brings to you DotSlash-an inception towards letting our innovations begin.


Our focus is on letting your imagination flow free and enable you to push the boundaries of what can be done. We're open for all! We'll take care of the food, travel and stay - virtually leaving you no excuse to not build something that'll blow our minds.

Go through the FAQ's so as to avoid perplexion.

Who can participate?

All students irrespective of any course is eligible to apply.If you are under 18 years of age, we'll need a parental consent form.


How does the application process work?

We’re looking for people who "do"! Folks who are passionate enough to work on crazy world-changing ideas. We’ll get to know more about your abilities from the past projects, GitHub profile, participation/awards in other hackathons.


Can we apply as a team?

Yes! This hackathon is strictly a team competition.You can join or create a team once you've started an individual application.Your team must consist of three members of same college. Most teams aim to have a mix of people with both design and developer skills.


What do I get by attending DotSlash?

Pretty much everything from socializing with fellow hacker communities. And yeah we’ll have prizes for 1st, 2nd, 3rd place, as well as the best hardware hack. Most of all, you will gain an unparalleled learning experience and memories for a lifetime!


What do I need to bring after registration?

A valid college issued photo ID, laptop, phone, chargers, any sleeping equipment you might want (we'll be providing mattresses) and a geeky t-shirt!


What shouldn’t I bring?

Don't bring anything which can get you in trouble like weapons of any kind, drugs, or alcohol. If you’re not sure whether something will be okay, please ask ahead of time!


What if I don’t have a team or an idea?

Not to worry, most people don’t! We’ll have team formation and ideation events geared towards helping you find people to work with. You can join our Facebook Group to find potential teammates.


Do we provide travel reimbursements?

Yes! Once accepted, you will have to submit your travel reimbursement forms. For each location hackers travel from, we’ll determine a reasonable cap. We will reimburse travel costs up to this cap only.


What if I've never been to a hackathon before?

Then we're so glad DotSlash will be your first ever! It’s helpful to have some programming or technical experience, but it’s not a requirement. We’ll have talks, mentors and workshops to help you with your project.


What about hardware?

If you have specific hardware in mind, bring it with you. We will be doing our best to lend hardware at the event and enable you to work on your dream hack. We’ll put out a list closer to the event so you can see what’s available.
